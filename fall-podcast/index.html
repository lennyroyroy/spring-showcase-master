Fall Podcasting

Our Fall Podcast crew worked hard to bring you 6 original episodes rangining from gtopics from Memes, to Life In Middle school to fictional stories and much much more. These 6 episodes are the product of 4 months of hard work and we are extremely proud to finally bring them to a wider audience!

Our students did all the research for these projects, recorded and re-recorded all the clips, and learned how to use the professional grade hardware like the Zoom H5 as well as exposure to editing software such as Audacity and Audition.

Hope you enjoy the show!
